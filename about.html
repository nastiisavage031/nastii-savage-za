About

Nastii Savage ZA (born December 26, 1995) is a multi-talented creative force hailing from Klerksdorp, South Africa. A producer, graphic designer, and lyricist, Nastii Savage brings a raw, authentic energy to everything he creates. His unique blend of sonic innovation and visual storytelling reflects his deep passion for the arts and a relentless drive to push boundaries. With an ear for powerful beats, a pen for sharp lyrics, and an eye for compelling visuals, Nastii Savage ZA continues to make his mark as a rising voice in the South African creative scene.
